Privacy Policy for DonkeySpotter

Last updated: 01/09/2025

1. Information We Collect

DonkeySpotter collects the following information:

  • Location data (when you report a donkey sighting)
  • Photos you upload of donkeys
  • Device identifier for reporting purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display donkey sighting locations on the map
  • Share donkey photos with other users
  • Manage inappropriate content reports
  • Improve the app's functionality

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, Google's cloud platform. Photos and location data are stored only as long as necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Sharing

We share donkey sighting information publicly within the app. This includes:

  • Sighting location
  • Timestamp
  • Associated photos

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Report inappropriate content
  • Request deletion of your sighting reports
  • Opt out of location services
