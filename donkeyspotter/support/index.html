Getting Started

DonkeySpotter is an app designed to help the UCR community track and share donkey sightings on campus. Here's how to use the app:

  • Enable location services to see nearby donkey sightings
  • Tap the "Report" button to share a new donkey sighting
  • Take a photo of the donkey
  • Confirm the location and submit your report

Common Issues

Location Services

If you're having trouble with location services:

  • Check if location services are enabled for DonkeySpotter in your device settings
  • Try restarting the app

Photo Upload Issues

If you're having trouble uploading photos:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure DonkeySpotter has permission to access your camera
